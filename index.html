Privacy Policy

Incremental ELM Quest 2 · Last updated: June 3, 2026

Short version: this app does not collect, store, or share any personal data.

Information we collect

None. Incremental ELM Quest 2 does not collect, transmit, or have access to any personal information or usage data. The app contains no analytics, no advertising, and no tracking of any kind.

Data stored on your device

Your game progress and settings are saved locally on your device only. This information never leaves your device, is not transmitted anywhere, and is not accessible to the developer. Deleting the app removes this data.

Game Center (optional)

If you choose to sign in to Apple Game Center, Apple may process your Game Center player ID and leaderboard scores to display leaderboards. This is handled entirely by Apple under Apple's Privacy Policy; the developer does not receive your identity or personal information. Game Center is optional and the game is fully playable without it.

In-app purchases

The game offers optional purchases that are processed by Apple through the App Store. The developer does not collect or receive your payment information; all transactions are handled by Apple under Apple's Privacy Policy. Purchases are optional and the game can be completed for free.

Children's privacy

The app does not collect personal information from anyone, including children. We do not knowingly collect data from children under the age of 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in your jurisdiction).

Third-party services

The app uses only Apple's own frameworks (such as Game Center and the App Store). It contains no third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking software.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new "Last updated" date.
