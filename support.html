Will my progress carry over if I switch devices or reinstall?
Your progress is saved on your device and is included in your encrypted iPhone backups, so it can carry over when you restore or migrate to a new device. There is no cloud sync yet, so a fresh install or deleting the app starts a new game. Cloud sync is on the roadmap for a future update.

Do I have to pay for anything?
No. The whole game is free, ad-free, and fully completable without spending. The optional purchases are a tip jar that supports development. If a purchase did not go through or did not grant its reward, email me with your App Store receipt and I will make it right.
